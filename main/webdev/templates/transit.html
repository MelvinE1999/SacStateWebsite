General Information

The City of Sacramento offers the Regional Transit for those who are looking for other forms transportation. Two forms of transportation SacRT currently offers to the Sac State campus are bus and light rail. With a purchase of a commuter sleeve, students can use the bus or light rail to commute to campus for free.

The following table provides more information on costs, and run times (as well as sustainanility factors) for for both forms of transportation.
